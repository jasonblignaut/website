OCD Assessment

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD) Test

Based on the OCI-R (Foa et al., 2002), this test assesses OCD symptoms across 6 domains: washing, checking, ordering, obsessing, hoarding, and neutralizing. Scores range from 0-72, with ≥21 indicating probable OCD.

Instructions: Answer each question based on your experiences over the past month. Select the option that best reflects how much the symptom has distressed or bothered you (0 = Not at all, 4 = Extremely). Your score will be calculated and visualized, with guidance on next steps.

Autism Assessment

Autism Spectrum Quotient (AQ) Test

Based on Baron-Cohen et al. (2001), this 50-item test measures autistic traits in adults across social skills, attention switching, attention to detail, communication, and imagination. Scores range from 0-50, with ≥32 indicating potential autism spectrum traits.

Instructions: For each statement, select how strongly it applies to you (Definitely Agree = 1, Slightly Agree = 1, Slightly Disagree = 0, Definitely Disagree = 0 for positive traits; reverse for negative). Your score will be calculated and visualized, with guidance on next steps.

High IQ Assessment

High IQ Test

Based on WAIS-IV principles, this 20-item test assesses logical reasoning, verbal comprehension, spatial skills, and working memory for high cognitive ability (IQ ≥ 130, top 2%). Scores are standardized to a mean of 100, SD of 15.

Instructions: Answer each question to the best of your ability. No time limit, but aim to complete within 30 minutes. Use a calculator or paper if needed. Your score will be calculated and visualized, indicating your cognitive potential.

Low IQ Assessment

Low IQ Test

Based on TONI-4 (Test of Nonverbal Intelligence), this 15-item test assesses nonverbal reasoning and problem-solving for individuals with potential cognitive challenges (IQ ≤ 70). Scores are standardized to a mean of 100, SD of 15.

Instructions: Select the option that best completes each pattern or solves the problem. No time limit, but aim to complete within 20 minutes. Your score will be calculated and visualized, with guidance on next steps.
