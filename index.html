Flower of Life Geometry

Exploring Terrence Howard's Concept

This interactive visualization demonstrates how overlapping circles (symbolizing magnetic fields) form geometric shapes like tetrahedrons, supporting the idea that the universe's structure arises from expansion and contraction. Adjust the number of circles to see the negative space (e.g., dark matter or proton geometry) emerge, as Howard suggests in his Flower of Life theory. The animation shows how these shapes balance out, challenging the straight-line approximations of traditional math.
